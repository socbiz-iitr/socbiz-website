About SocBiz IITR

Society of Business, IIT Roorkee (SocBiz IITR) is a premier student organization dedicated to developing proficient problem solvers and future business leaders in Consulting, Product Management, and Data Analytics. Established in 2009 and rebranded in 2023, SocBiz IITR fosters an environment that encourages the exploration and application of business acumen to real-world challenges.

We collaborate with industry leaders, engage in impactful projects, and offer a platform for students to learn, experiment, and excel. Our initiatives include comprehensive training modules, workshops with industry experts, and participation in national and international competitions.

Our Verticals

Training, case competitions, and real-life projects to prepare students for careers in management consulting. Members have worked on projects like organizational analysis and market evaluation for global firms.
Structured curricula, case competitions, and hands-on projects. Members have contributed to startups like Tvelp and CoverSure, focusing on marketing strategy and user engagement.
Preparing students for data-driven decision-making roles through workshops, analytics challenges, and collaborative research projects with industry and academia.

Achievements & Legacy

  • Winners in global case competitions and Inter-IIT awards (Gold in Analytics Challenge 2023, Silver and Bronze in Product and Analytics).
  • Collaborative research projects, including automation for criminal investigation and decarbonization trends with global companies.
  • Hosted flagship events like Business Baazigar and Pan India Case Competition with 3,000+ participants.
